Information About me

I started learning web development about a year ago. After working on some informal projects on my own, I read a newspaper article about Holberton Tulsa. It seemed like a great fit and an amazing opportunity for me. I've enjoyed my time at Holberton and have certainly learned a lot. I have completed coursework using C, Python, bash scripts, MySQL, React JS, and TypeScript, among others. I decided to focus on Front-End web development because I have previously worked in customer service roles and Front-End made the most logical sense with my previous employment experiences.

I can't say I have a favorite genre of movie but I will never turn down a Sci-Fi/Fantasy movie or TV show. I play golf infrequently but whenever I feel like embarassing myself, I go play. Other than that, I enjoy spending time with family and friends.
